Definitions:
- 3LC: 3 letter code (typically for cities)

standard_no_press.jsonl:
- Each entry contains keys 'id', 'map', 'rules', 'phases'
    - 'map' should be standard
- 'phases': a list of dicts with keys 'name', 'state', 'orders', 'results', 'messages'
- 'state': a dict with important keys 'units', 'centers', 'homes', 'influence', 'civil_disorder', 'builds', 'game_id', 'map', 'rules', 'retreats'
    - 'units': dict mapping nations to lists of A or F for army / fleet respectively, followed by 3LC city.
    - 'centers': dict mapping nations to lists of supply centers
    - 'homes': ?
    - 'influence': dict mapping nations to lists of 3LC cities last occupied.
    - 'civil_disorder': only important when a player leaves the game
    - 'builds': ?
